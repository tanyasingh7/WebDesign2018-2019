After 9/11, there have been 300 documented cases of violence and discrimination against Sikh Americans throughout the US The image of Sikhs - Turbans, long hair, long bears, etc - has become misconceived and has spurred feelings of Islamophobia, racism, and discrimination. The number of hate crimes is on the rise and intolerance has become the norm. Sikh Americans face school bullying, workplace discrimination, racial and religious profiling, and much more.
